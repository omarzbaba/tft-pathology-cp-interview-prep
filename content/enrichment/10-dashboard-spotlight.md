# Dashboard Spotlight — Strongest Sections to Feature

When this corpus becomes a dashboard, these are the sections to surface first — the highest-signal, most interview-ready material. Ranked by a blend of role-relevance (core lab management, informatics, integration), interview yield, and how directly the learner can *use* it under pressure.

## Tier 1 — Feature prominently (the spine of the prep)

| Section | Why it's a spotlight | Best dashboard treatment |
|---|---|---|
| **Core Lab Management** (`04e` + `04e` enrichment) | The role's #1 named need; deepest, most realistic management content, now with dialogue-level examples and a "Management depth" block. | Hero card / first chapter. Surface the management dialogue snippets and the urgent-vs-important examples. |
| **Multi-Hospital Integration** (`04j` + enrichment) | Directly maps to BILH growth; the differentiator most candidates miss. | Featured card tagged "BILH-specific." Pull the "when NOT to harmonize" nuance. |
| **Interview Bank + Sharpening** (`06` parts 1–3 + `06-interview-sharpening` 1–2) | 44 questions with model/weaker answers, plus conversational concise + nuanced versions. The core drill surface. | "Drill mode": show question → reveal sharpened answer → concise/nuanced toggle. |
| **Say This, Not That — Master Library** (`07-say-this-not-that-library`) | Highest quick-win-per-minute; instantly makes answers sound mature and collaborative. | Filterable phrase bank by domain; "night-before" quick-reference. |
| **Cram Upgrade** (`09-cram-upgrade` + base `09`) | One-page high-yield, top-5/three-things per chapter, most-likely-asked and most-impressive markers. | "Cram mode" / 20- and 60-minute timed paths. |

## Tier 2 — Strong supporting features

| Section | Why | Treatment |
|---|---|---|
| **Project Bank** (`05` parts 1–2 + deepening + `05-part-3` new archetypes = 30 archetypes) | Story stock across every CP domain; junior vs senior framing and "if I didn't lead it" honesty. | Filter by domain/competency; show 30-sec + 2-min toggles. |
| **Speaking Scripts** (`07`) | Rehearsal reps for the openers (60/90-sec intro, why-this-role, why-BILH). | Audio-rehearsal cards with word counts and pause points. |
| **Plain-English Master** (`08-plain-english-master`) | Kills jargon on the phone; four versions per concept. | Flashcard-style; "elevator / interview-safe" toggle. |
| **LIS & Interoperability** (`04b`, `04c` + enrichment) | The informatics half of the role, now de-jargoned with concrete examples. | Concept cards with the plain-English lead-in first, jargon held in reserve. |

## Tier 3 — Depth on demand (search/reference)

- Foundations (`04a`), Workflow/Operations (`04d`), Quality/Validation (`04f`), Digital Pathology (`04g`), Analytics/CDS (`04h`), Leadership/Change (`04i`) and their enrichment packs — excellent for targeted study and follow-up prep, best surfaced via search and tags rather than front-and-center.
- Glossary (`10`), Self-Test (`11`) — reference and self-quiz utilities.
- Audit & Gap Analysis (`00-audit-gap-analysis`) — meta/internal; useful for the dashboard builder, not the learner.

A "**night before**" view that combines: the Say-This/Not-That library, the Cram Upgrade one-pager, the top interview-sharpening answers, and the two intro scripts. That bundle is what the learner will actually open in the final hour.
